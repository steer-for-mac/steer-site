Privacy Policy

Effective 10 April 2026

Summary

Steer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Controller input is processed entirely on-device and never leaves your Mac.

Data collected

None. No usage data, no crash reports, no analytics of any kind.

Network connections

Steer makes no network connections during normal use. Controller input never leaves your Mac. Purchase and licensing details are being finalised ahead of launch; this page will be updated to describe exactly what connects, and when, before the app ships.

Permissions

Steer requests one macOS permission, plus an optional second:

  • Accessibility — required to move the cursor and dispatch keyboard events on your behalf.
  • Input Monitoring — only requested if you use a DualSense Edge and want to bind its two rear paddles. Every other controller works with Accessibility alone.

These permissions are used exclusively to translate controller input into Mac input events. No input data is recorded or transmitted.
