Privacy Policy

Effective 10 April 2026

Summary

Steer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Controller input is processed entirely on-device and never leaves your Mac.

Data collected

None. No usage data, no crash reports, no analytics of any kind.

Network connections

Steer makes no network connections during normal use. The only exception is at the time of purchase or when restoring a purchase, which is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.

iCloud

The App Store version of Steer writes a single timestamp to your personal iCloud Key-Value Storage — the date your free trial began. This is used only to prevent the trial from restarting on reinstall. No other data is written to iCloud.

Permissions

Steer requests two macOS permissions:

  • Accessibility — required to move the cursor and dispatch keyboard events on your behalf.
  • Input Monitoring — required to read controller input at the system level.

These permissions are used exclusively to translate controller input into Mac input events. No input data is recorded or transmitted.
