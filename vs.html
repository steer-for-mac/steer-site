If you're choosing, choose with the facts.

Both apps are notarized direct downloads. ControllerKeys documents that it runs outside Apple's sandbox because system-wide remapping needs broad permissions; Steer keeps the sandbox anyway.

Off-brand controller support and Elite rear-paddle reading are both written and in beta. Steer bundles 300+ SDL controller mappings of its own, but none of them has been confirmed on real hardware yet, so it stays in beta rather than claiming parity. We're looking for testers.

Checked against ControllerKeys v2.6.0 on 19 July 2026. They ship often, so check their site too.
