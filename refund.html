Mac App Store purchases

How to request a refund

Steer is sold exclusively through the Mac App Store. Refunds are handled by Apple, not by the developer.

To request a refund:

  • Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with your Apple ID
  • Find the Steer purchase and select "Request a Refund"

Trial

Steer includes a 7-day free trial before any payment is required. If the app doesn't work for your setup, you can uninstall during the trial at no cost.

Having trouble?

If Steer isn't working as expected, get in touch before requesting a refund — most issues can be resolved quickly.
