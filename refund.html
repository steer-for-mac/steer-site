Refunds

Steer has not shipped yet, so this is how refunds will work. A direct purchase with a 14-day free trial, so you can be sure before you pay. If you still want a refund after buying, it is a quick email, with no forms.

Trial first

Steer includes a 14-day free trial with full functionality before any payment is required. If it doesn't work for your setup, uninstall during the trial at no cost.

How to request a refund

Email steer@seanfloyd.dev with the email address you bought with (or your order number) and I'll issue the refund. No forms, no justification required.

How it's processed

Steer is sold through Lemon Squeezy, which is legally the seller; their name is what appears on your card statement. I issue refunds through their dashboard, and the money returns to your original payment method. You can also contact Lemon Squeezy support directly with your order details.

Having trouble?

If Steer isn't working as expected, get in touch before requesting a refund; most issues can be resolved quickly.
