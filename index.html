Cursor & Scroll

125Hz polling, configurable acceleration curves, sub-pixel precision. Works like a trackpad, not a d-pad.

Layers & Shortcuts

Four modifier layers per profile. Map any button to keyboard shortcuts, media controls, or system actions.

Per-App Profiles

Automatically switch profiles when your active app changes. One config for browsers, another for video.

Gyro Mouse

Use the DualSense or DualShock 4 gyroscope for precision cursor control — hold a button to activate.

Haptics & Triggers

Haptic feedback on events like scroll boundaries and focus changes. Adaptive trigger effects on DualSense.

Radial Menu

Stick-controlled quick-launch menu. Flick to an app, release to open. Configurable layout and items.
